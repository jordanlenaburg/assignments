V School hits super awesome record!

V School broke a world record today by becoming the first Coding Boot Camp to accept cats into it's body of students. The April 24th class, included 10 humans and 3 cats.

"We think cats are the future of coding."

"We are really excited to offer this opportunity to our feline friends," says Bob Ziroll, Lead Instructor at V School. "We really think cats are the future of coding. I personally interview every student before they are accepted to V School, so I see the raw talent each person offers. These cats are amazing!"

While it remains to be seen if V School's new policy is crazy or genius, it is certianlly attracting a lot of attention. "You've got to give them credit for innovation," said Mark Anthony of DevPoint Labs, a competing school.

Regardless of whether or not the felines pass the course, one thing will always remain true: Cats rule the internet.
